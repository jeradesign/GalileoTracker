Laplace

What You’re Seeing:
This demo takes the Laplace operator (also known as the Laplacian) on each point for the primary colors red, green and blue. The Laplacian is defined as the sum of the second derivative in the x and y directions.

How It Works:
In OpenCV, the Laplacian function uses the Sobel operator to produce the second derivatives with respect to x and y, and then adds them together.

What It’s Used For:
The Laplace operator performs a simple form of edge detection. Edges appear as black lines surrounded by white areas.

Try and Notice:
Point the camera at an object of a solid color. Note how the center of the object appears black, while the edges are the color of the the object.
